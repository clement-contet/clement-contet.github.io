    <h2>About</h2>
    <hr class="w3-opacity">
    <p><i> I am finishing my PhD in Summer 2023 and I am open to work after my PhD. If you are interested in my profile, please reach out!</i></p>
    <p> I am a final-year PhD student at PSL University (Université Paris Dauphine) and Meta AI (Facebook AI Research), advised by <a href="https://scholar.google.fr/citations?user=tYro5N8AAAAJ&hl">Nicolas Usunier</a> (Meta AI), <a href="https://www.lamsade.dauphine.fr/~atif/doku.php">Jamal Atif</a> and <a href="https://fr.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jérôme_Lang">Jérôme Lang</a> (PSL University, CNRS). I am based in Paris, France.</p>

    <p> I am interested in machine learning, social choice and responsible AI. My PhD focuses on fairness in recommender systems. An overview can be found in this <a href="https://montrealethics.ai/fair-allocation-of-exposure-in-recommender-systems/">blogpost</a>.</p>

    <p> Prior to my PhD, I received an MSc in Social Data Science from the University of Oxford, an MSc/BSc in Applied Mathematics from Ecole Polytechnique <i>(Diplôme d'Ingénieur)</i> , and a BA in Philosophy from Université Paris Nanterre. </p>
